# Ledger

Small B2B finance tool. Replaces the spreadsheet that small ops teams use to reconcile vendor invoices against bank transactions.

## Stage

Pre-revenue. 2 pilot customers. Targeting v1 launch in 6 weeks.

## Stack

- Postgres on Fly.io (decided 2026-04-15, see [[2026-04-15 — vendor pick]])
- Next.js + tRPC
- Stripe for billing (deferred until v1)

## Open decisions

- Multi-currency: deferred to v2 (decided 2026-04-09).
- Pricing: $99/mo seat-based vs $499/mo flat. Lean flat — pilots prefer predictability.
